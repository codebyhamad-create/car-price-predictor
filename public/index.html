The Process

How the Model Works

From raw data to price prediction — a transparent look at the machine learning pipeline.

01
Data Collection

1,276 Indian car listings were collected spanning 40 brands, covering economy hatchbacks to ultra-luxury sedans and SUVs.

02
Feature Engineering

Raw data like power ratings and dimensions were parsed, cleaned, and transformed into 18 structured numeric features including brand goodwill tier.

03
Model Training

A Gradient Boosting Regressor was trained on 80% of the data with 400 estimators, learning non-linear relationships between specs and price.

04
Evaluation

On the held-out test set, the model achieved R² = 0.981, meaning it explains 98.1% of price variance — well above the overfitting threshold.
